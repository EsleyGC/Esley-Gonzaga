About Me

Greetings! I'm Esley Gonzaga, a passionate game developer fueled by a love for crafting immersive digital experiences. My journey in game development began in 2013, delving into the creation of engaging fighting games. Since 2019, I've transitioned to utilizing the Unity engine for professional game development, progressing from a hobbyist to a committed gameplay programmer.


In the realm of game development, my focus centers on mastering the intricacies of the 3Cs – Character, Controls, and Camera. From fine-tuning movement systems to orchestrating complex combat mechanics, I find fulfillment in the challenge of crafting gameplay that captivates and entertains.


My expertise extends beyond programming; My proficiency in both animation and game design not only allows me to seamlessly collaborate within a multidisciplinary team but also empowers me to translate creative visions into enjoyable gameplay mechanics, ensuring a harmonious fusion of technical precision and player engagement.


Additionally, I'm expanding my toolkit by delving into the Unreal Engine, adding another layer to my skill set.
